Exercises: Spelling and Pronunciation

Exercise 1: Commonly Confused Words

Choose the correct word to complete each sentence.

1. Please __________________ (accept/except) my apology for being late.

accept

2. The weather had a significant __________________ (affect/effect) on our travel plans.

effect

3. I'm not sure __________________ (weather/whether) to go to the party or not.

whether

4. She couldn't __________________ (bare/bear) to watch the sad ending of the film.

bear

5. The company will __________________ (lose/loose) customers if they don't improve their service.

lose

6. The __________________ (principal/principle) of the school gave a speech at the graduation ceremony.

principal

7. You should __________________ (complement/compliment) her on her presentation.

compliment

8. The __________________ (stationary/stationery) store sells pens, notebooks, and other office supplies.

stationery

Exercise 3: Homophones

Choose the correct homophone to complete each sentence.

1. The ship will __________________ (sail/sale) at dawn.

sail

2. She needs to __________________ (heal/heel) from her injury before returning to sports.

heal

3. The __________________ (main/mane) reason for his success is his determination.

main

4. I need to __________________ (write/right) a letter to my grandmother.

write

5. The __________________ (knight/night) sky was filled with stars.

night

6. We need to __________________ (wait/weight) for the bus.

wait

7. The __________________ (flour/flower) garden was beautiful in spring.

flower

8. I can __________________ (hear/here) the music from next door.

hear

Exercise 5: Spelling Rules

Apply the correct spelling rule to form the word in each sentence.

1. She __________________ (study + ing) for her exams all week.

studying (keep the 'y' when adding 'ing')

2. The children were __________________ (run + ing) around the playground.

running (double the final consonant when adding 'ing')

3. She is the __________________ (happy + est) person I know.

happiest (change 'y' to 'i' when adding 'est')

4. The __________________ (achieve + ment) of his goals took years of hard work.

achievement (drop the final 'e' when adding a suffix beginning with a vowel)

5. The __________________ (care + ful) driver avoided the accident.

careful (keep the final 'e' when adding a suffix beginning with a consonant)
