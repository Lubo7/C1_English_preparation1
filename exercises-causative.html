Exercises: Causative Structures

Practice your understanding of causative structures with these exercises. Test your knowledge of have/get something done, make/let/have someone do something, and other causative forms.

Back to Exercises
Review Causative Structures Grammar

Exercise 1: Gap Fill

Complete each sentence with the correct form of have/get something done.

1. I need to __________________ before the wedding next month. (my hair/cut)

have/get my hair cut

2. They __________________ last week after the storm damaged it. (their roof/repair)

had/got their roof repaired

3. How often do you __________________ ? (your eyes/test)

have/get your eyes tested

4. She __________________ tomorrow morning. (her car/service)

is having/getting her car serviced

5. We __________________ next week. (the house/paint)

are having/getting the house painted

Exercise 2: Sentence Transformation

Rewrite each sentence using a causative structure (have/get something done or make/let/have someone do something).

1. The dentist checked my teeth yesterday.

→ I ...

I had/got my teeth checked (by the dentist) yesterday.

2. The teacher told the students to finish their homework.

→ The teacher ...

The teacher made the students finish their homework.

3. Someone is going to clean our windows tomorrow.

→ We ...

We are having/getting our windows cleaned tomorrow.

4. My parents allowed me to stay out late.

→ My parents ...

My parents let me stay out late.

5. A professional photographer took our wedding photos.

→ We ...

We had/got our wedding photos taken by a professional photographer.

Exercise 3: Multiple Choice

Choose the correct causative form to complete each sentence.

1. The manager __________________ early to prepare for the meeting.

had his assistant come

2. I need to __________________ before the interview.

get my suit cleaned

3. The teacher __________________ their homework during the lunch break.

let the students finish

4. They __________________ by a famous designer.

had their house decorated

5. The director __________________ the scene several times.

made the actors rehearse

Exercise 4: Error Correction

Each sentence below contains an error in the use of causative structures. Identify and correct the error.

1. I had my car repaired it yesterday.

I had my car repaired yesterday. (Remove "it")

2. She made him to clean the kitchen.

She made him clean the kitchen. (Remove "to")

3. We let the children playing in the garden.

We let the children play in the garden. (Change "playing" to "play")

4. They had been cut their hair before the wedding.

They had had their hair cut before the wedding. (Change "had been cut" to "had had...cut")

5. I'm going to have clean my apartment tomorrow.

I'm going to have my apartment cleaned tomorrow. (Change "have clean my apartment" to "have my apartment cleaned")

Exercise 5: Complete the Sentences

Complete these sentences using causative structures. There may be more than one possible answer.

1. I don't cut my own hair. I always __________________ .

have/get it cut (by a professional)

2. The boss is very strict. He __________________ overtime when necessary.

makes everyone work

3. My phone was broken, so I __________________ yesterday.

had/got it repaired

4. The children were excited because their parents __________________ to the zoo.

let them go

5. Before moving into our new house, we __________________ throughout.

had/got it painted
